Industrial Training:

During my industrial training, I gained hands-on experience working with real-world information management tasks. I learned how to organize, document, and archive data systematically. The training provided valuable insight into professional work environments and helped me understand the practical applications of my theoretical studies.

Creative Media & Infographics:

I was involved in designing infographics and creative content for school and community events. Using tools like Canva, I developed visually appealing materials that effectively communicated information. This experience helped me refine my design sense, time management and attention to audience needs. I also learned how to collaborate with peers to deliver quality content within tight deadlines which enhanced my project management skills.
